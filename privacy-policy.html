PRIVACY POLICY

OBS Publisher provides this Privacy Policy to inform users of our policies and procedures regarding the collection, use and disclosure of personally identifiable information received from users of OBS Publisher's websites, located at the domains: Hellskitchenrecipes.com, Homechefninja.com, Uberkitchn.com, Preparedforthat.com (the "OBS Publisher Websites"). Please note that this Privacy Policy only applies to the OBS Publisher Websites.

Information Collection and Use

It is unnecessary to provide OBS Publisher with your personally identifiable information to use the OBS Publisher Websites. Unless you subscribe to an OBS Publisher newsletter, OBS Publisher does not collect personally identifiable information concerning the operation of the OBS Publisher Websites. Suppose you contact OBS Publisher by email through the OBS Publisher Websites. In that case, OBS Publisher may keep a record of your contact information and correspondence and may use your email address and any information you provide to OBS Publisher in your message to respond to you. OBS Publisher treats this information as it would treat any other unsolicited business correspondence and has no obligation to keep it confidential or take any security measures to protect it.

If you subscribe to an OBS Publisher newsletter, you must provide us with your email address. We will use the email address you provide to subscribe to our newsletter(s) to send you the newsletter(s), marketing or promotional opportunities from us and our partners we believe may interest you, and certain administrative communications regarding the newsletters. We will not sell or rent your email address to anyone else for their marketing. You may unsubscribe from any or all of our newsletters or marketing communications at any time using the link provided in the email, and we will delete your email address from our records.

When you visit an OBS Publisher Website, OBS Publisher's servers automatically record information that your browser sends whenever you visit a website ("Log Data"). This Log Data may include information such as your IP address, browser type or the domain from which you are visiting. For most users accessing the Internet from an Internet service provider, the IP address will differ every time you log on. OBS Publisher uses Log Data to monitor the use of the OBS Publisher Websites and the services we offer via the OBS Publisher Websites and for the OBS Publisher Website's technical administration.

Cookies and Other Analytic Tools

Like many websites, OBS Publisher Websites also use "cookie" technology to collect additional website usage data and to improve the OBS Publisher Website and the services offered via the Web Site. A cookie is a small data file that OBS Publisher's servers transfer to your computer's hard disk. OBS Publisher may also use tracking pixels. Tracking pixels are small graphic images used in email messages to determine whether the messages were opened and the links were clicked or are used on the OBS Publisher Websites to collect information about the browser and device viewing the OBS Publisher Websites. OBS Publisher does not use cookies or tracking pixels to collect personally identifiable information.

OBS Publisher mainly uses "session cookies," which enable certain features of the OBS Publisher Websites and services offered via the OBS Publisher Websites to understand better how you interact with the Websites and services we offer via the OBS Publisher Websites, to monitor aggregate usage by our users and web traffic routing on the OBS Publisher Websites, and to improve the OBS Publisher Websites and services offered via the OBS Publisher Websites. Session cookies should be deleted from your computer when you disconnect or leave the OBS Publisher Websites.

OBS Publisher also uses a persistent cookie on some of the OBS Publisher Websites that stay on your computer for some time after you leave the OBS Publisher Websites for the sole purpose of ensuring that where rating, voting, or survey functions are available, you do not participate more than once.
